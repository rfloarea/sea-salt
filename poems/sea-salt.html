sea/salt

An ache live between my ribs.

The sea has always pulled it out of me

the salt of me // the salt of water

The only place I know to be from.


This ache, my constant companion

was planted in me centuries

before my lungs breathed

salt


Whe you chose, for me,

without me,

to rupture into so many pieces,

the bottom of the sea

was the only place that could

hold

us.
