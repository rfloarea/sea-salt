bury/sink

there is a well inside of me

it rises

to meet the tide


i want to bury

myself in soil

that knows me


i want to bury

us in soil

that knows us


there is a well inside of me

it stretches

to meet the tide


i want to go home

sink into soil

sink into salt

let the tide pull me

out and in

in and out

let the earth sink me

into her


i want to know the spirits

i want them to know me

i want to go home to soil


the sea remembers me
